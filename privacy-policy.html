秘密の箱 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-28

秘密の箱 is a privacy-first local media vault for storing videos, images, and audio on the user's device. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is protected.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

The developer does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data, media files, backup contents, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data from this app.

  • No account registration is provided.
  • No analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs are included.
  • No tracking is performed.
  • User media is not sent to developer-operated servers.

2. Data Stored on the Device

Videos, images, audio, albums, favorites, trash state, app settings, and Secret Vault metadata are stored locally on the device. Secret Vault content is handled separately from the normal vault inside the app.

3. Permissions

The app may request access to the photo library or media picker so the user can import selected media into the local vault. The app may also use device authentication features such as Face ID, Touch ID, biometric authentication, or PIN-based unlock flows when enabled by the user.

4. Backup and Restore

Backup and restore are manual user actions. Backup files are created by the app and saved to a location chosen by the user. Backup files may be encrypted with a user-provided password. The developer does not receive or store backup passwords and cannot recover a backup if the password is lost.

5. Purchases

The app may offer a one-time Pro unlock through the App Store or Google Play billing systems. Purchase processing, payment information, and store account handling are managed by Apple or Google. The app stores only the local entitlement state needed to unlock Pro features.

6. Third Parties

The app does not use third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, cloud sync, or remote account services. Store purchase flows are provided by Apple or Google when the user chooses to buy or restore Pro.

7. User Control

Users can delete media, restore items from trash where supported, remove app data by uninstalling the app, and manage system permissions through iOS or Android settings. Data exported by the user, such as backup files, is controlled by the user and the destination they choose.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when app behavior changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page before release.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the project issue page: https://github.com/aoki1309-jpg/himitsu-box1309/issues.

This policy applies to the mobile app "秘密の箱" / "Himitsu Box".
